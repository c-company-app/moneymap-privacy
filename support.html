Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe? Where is it stored?
All data is stored on “your device only.” MoneyMap has no server and doesn’t send data off your device on its own — no behavioural analytics, no ads, no tracking. Read more in our Privacy Policy.
Do I need to sign up or log in?
No. Just open the app and start — there are no user accounts inside the app.
How do I back up or move my data to a new phone?
Go to Settings → Export/Backup → “Encrypted backup” to create a backup file, then “Restore” it on the new device · or export a CSV to open in Excel/Google Sheets.
How does Sync to Google Sheet work?
It’s optional and off by default. When you tap Sync, the app asks you to sign in with Google (only then) and writes your records to a Google Sheet in your own Google account, requesting access only to the file the app creates — never through our server.
I forgot my encrypted backup passphrase. What now?
Because the file is encrypted with your own passphrase and we have no server storing it, a forgotten passphrase means the file can’t be recovered. Please keep it somewhere safe.
Is the app free? What languages are supported?
It’s free. It supports Thai and English, has light and dark themes, and works fully offline.
Can I lock the app with Face ID?
Yes — go to Settings → Security to enable app lock with Face ID / Touch ID / your device passcode.
